Brief Introduction

Welcome to Stock Funnel, Here we will funnel stock for you based on your invest type or if you are an experienced investor, you can use the Stock Funnel to choose stock that meet your requirement.

In the stock funnel you need to fill the user survey so we can get your investment type. After that, you can filter the stocks according to the indicators you want and the system will return you stocks that may interest you based on your input and your investment type. Of course, you can also use the search function to freely view the data you need.
